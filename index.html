About Project RelayX

Project RelayX is a new approach to secure, private communication, born out of the belief that privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature you should pay for with your data. Unlike corporate messaging platforms that profit from surveillance capitalism, RelayX operates on a fully open-source, non-profit model. Our architecture is meticulously crafted from the ground up to ensure true end-to-end encryption, guaranteeing No Metadata and that your conversations remain confidential.

Every line of code is freely auditable by the community, providing transparency and establishing a trustworthy standard for security. We bypass traditional centralized servers by utilizing a cutting-edge decentralized relay network, making it significantly harder for any single entity to monitor communications or enforce censorship. The core funding model, explicitly **"Funded by You,"** ensures that our sole accountability is to our users, not to investors or advertisers. This commitment to user control drives every decision we make. Project RelayX is a community-driven initiative dedicated to providing a messaging solution that respects digital sovereignty, fostering a private digital ecosystem.

Project RelayX Features

End-to-End Encryption

Your messages are secured by industry-leading E2EE protocols. This means only you and the intended recipient can read your chats. We use cryptographic keys managed locally on your device RAM, ensuring that even we cannot access the content of your communications. Your privacy is our Committment.

Decentralized by Default

RelayX utilizes a distributed network architecture, eliminating reliance on any single, central server. This design maximizes resilience, prevents single points of failure, and makes it virtually impossible for third parties to enforce global shutdowns or mass surveillance on the network.

Open-Source & Auditable

The entire application source code is publicly available and freely auditable. This transparency allows security experts and the community to rigorously inspect our systems for vulnerabilities and verify our claims about privacy and encryption. Trust is earned, not just asked for.

No Ads, No Trackers

We guarantee a completely clean, tracker-free user experience. Since we are funded solely by the community, we have zero incentive to collect, analyze, or monetize your personal data or usage patterns. Your focus remains purely on communication.

Funded by You

Project RelayX is a community-supported non-profit. Our funding comes directly from users who believe in the mission of private communication. This model ensures that our priorities are always aligned with user security and never dictated by advertisers or commercial interests.

Cross-Platform

Stay connected regardless of your device. RelayX provides seamless and consistent functionality across desktop platforms (Windows, Linux) and mobile (Android). Experience full-featured, private messaging without being locked into a single operating system ecosystem.

Security Design Layers

Layer Description
Key-Pair Each user generates a deterministic private–public x25519 Ephemeral keypair using Cryptography
Handshake A shared Key, 2 Nonces are exchanged between 2 Users. A session Key is thus generated
Encryption Messages use SHA-256, AES-128, AEAD, x25519, Fernet, HKDF etc. for security.
Rotation Chat keys are rotated automatically every 10 minutes or 25 messages making it hard to crack.
Extra Each message includes a Timestamp and Associated Data to stop MitM and replay.
